Major Assignment 1 Revision Plan

If asked which Student Learning Outcome I would say I thought was most useful I would say it was Revision. I have dabbled in the arts from drawing, to music, to video game prodruction. In all of these I would always show it to some people and ask them for their opinion. I will usualy ask "what is something you like and something you dislike about [insert project here]" or at the very least I will ask for what their least favorite part is. Constructive criticism is very important to me, I know no matter how good I get that I can always be and do better. Someone saying their opinion, more importantly their negitive opinion, I can keep that in mind to try my best to avoid or expand on that next time.

When it comes to ENC 1101 for each of our Major Assignments we would have two or three days where we got feedback from fresh eyes weather that ge a peer or the prossessor himself. Each person in that class has different experiences and writing styles as explained in Varriation Across Contexts tab, so it is useful to hear from them what they think of what I made.
